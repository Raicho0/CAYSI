Tashkent

Nemolchi.uz is an independent information project against violence in Uzbekistan, established in 2017. It was created to draw attention to violence against women and to change society's attitude towards such an issue.

Now it is a platform where readers can find useful information about what violence is, how to fight violence, where to get help. Victims of gender-based violence can tell their stories anonymously and get psychological support.
