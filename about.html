About us

What is CAYSI?

Central Asian Youth Social Initiiatives

It is an online platform that connects student-run societies across Central Asia. It serves as a hub for youth to promote their projects, attract participants, and gain valuable recognition.

Why trust CAYSI?

Where is the support coming from?

CAYSI is supported by the Political Computer Science Student Association from UC Berkeley. These partnerships enhance the platform's credibility, providing valuable resources to young leaders across Central Asia.
